Hey! Looks like Mr Crab found a vault of vocabulary units! Let us open it and get acquainted with the words and phrases you can use in your article to sound good and professional!
    Introduction & rhetorical questions:
  1. Have you ever (thought about)…?
  2. Do you ever wonder…?
  3. What do you think about…?
  4. Are you a… like me?
  5. Are you one of those people who…?
  6. What would you say if I told you that…?
  7. What would you do if …?
  8. Do you like …?
  9. How important is it for you to …?
  10. What would life be like if …?
    Giving your opinion:
  1. In my opinion, …
  2. From my point of view, …
  3. As far as I’m concerned, …
  4. I guess that …
  5. I’m under the impression that …
  6. I have no doubt that …
  7. I have the feeling that…
